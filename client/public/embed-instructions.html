Embed the FootCare Chatbot

To embed the Fiona chatbot on your website, copy and paste the following code just before the closing </body> tag of your HTML:

<script>
  window.fcChat = window.fcChat || function () {
    (window.fcChat.q = window.fcChat.q || []).push(arguments);
  };
  fcChat('init');
</script>
<script src="https://footcareclinic.engageiobots.com/widget.js" defer></script>

This will add a floating Fiona chat button to your website. When clicked, it opens the chatbot window for visitors to interact with.
